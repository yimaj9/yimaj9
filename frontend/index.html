About

The Harari Construction and Infrastructure Works Enterprise was established in 2017 E.C. by the regional government to address growing housing and infrastructure challenges. As a government-owned institution, it aims to streamline construction efforts, close organizational gaps, and deliver development projects more efficiently. The enterprise focuses on meeting public demand, managing resources effectively, and competing in the open market

shenkor Corridor Development

The Shenkor Project officially began on 08/04/2017 E.C. and is estimated to be completed by the end of the same year. Covering approximately 870 meters of land, the project has a total budget of around 56 million ETB. The scope of work includes street construction using machinery, installation of pipelines, and related infrastructure development.

Abadir Road

Abadir Road has a length of 640 meters and a width of 24 meters. Starting From Abadir Plaza (Asmadin Beri) To (Suduq At Beri) Construction began in On May 2015 and was estimated to be completed by October 2025, with over 100 workers involved in the project. The total budget allocated was 105 million ETB.

The project was initiated to improve the road infrastructure in Harar, enhancing connectivity and accessibility for residents and visitors.

The road is designed to accommodate both vehicular and pedestrian traffic, featuring modern amenities such as street lighting and bicycle lanes to ensure safety and convenience.

Abadir Road is not just a transportation route; it also serves as a vital link between various parts of the city, promoting economic activities and community interactions.

  • Client: Transport & Road Development Bureau
  • Consultant: Transport & Road Development Bureau

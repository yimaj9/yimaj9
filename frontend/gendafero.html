Genda Fero Road

The Genda Fero Road Project is a 1 km long and 15-meter wide road construction starts from Argoberi all the way to the Gendafero River. Began in June 2025 and expected to be completed by November 30, 2025, the project has a total allocated budget of 119,419,404 ETB. It is designed to improve transportation access and boost local development in the area.
